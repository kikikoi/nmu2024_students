North Ming Zu University information North MinZu University information North Minzu University (abbreviated as NMU; Chinese: 北方民族大学; pinyin: Běifāng Mínzú Dàxúe) is a Chinese university located at Yinchuan City, Ningxia Province, China.[4] It is a comprehensive national institution of higher education which is affiliated to the State Ethnic Affairs Commission.[2][4]
